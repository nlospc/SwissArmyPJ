PROJECT CANVAS

目的 Purpose
?
为什么开展这个项目？解决的根本问题或机会是什么？
Why the project exists. The underlying problem or opportunity to be addressed.
🔭
目标 Objective
?
衡量项目成功的可量化结果。
Measurable outcomes indicating project success.
🎯
发起人 Sponsor
?
提供资源和支持，并对项目成功负责的个人或团体。
The person or group providing resources/support, accountable for enabling success.
💼
需求 Requirements
?
必须满足的高层级条件或能力。
High-level conditions or capabilities that must be met.
📋
可交付物 Deliverables
?
产生的有形或无形的产品、结果或能力。
Tangible or intangible products, results, or capabilities produced.
🎁
范围之外 Exclusions
?
明确指出不包含的内容，以防止范围蔓延。
Explicit boundaries of what is NOT included to prevent scope creep.
🚫
制约/假设 Constraints
?
视为真实的因素，以及影响执行的限制因素（预算、时间）。
Factors considered true without proof, and limiting factors affecting execution.
🔒
项目阶段 Phases
?
逻辑相关的活动集合，以完成特定成果为目标。
A collection of logically related activities culminating in major deliverables.
🔄
里程碑 Milestones
?
项目进度中的重要时间点或重大事件。
Significant points or events in the project timeline.
🚩
资源 Resources
?
执行项目所需的资金、设备、材料或设施。
Funding, equipment, materials, or facilities required to perform the work.
⛵
风险 Risks
?
一旦发生会对项目目标产生积极或消极影响的不确定事件。
Uncertain events that, if they occur, have an effect on project objectives.
🦈
团队 Team
?
直接执行工作以实现项目目标的内部成员。
Individuals directly performing the work to achieve project objectives.
🧑‍🤝‍🧑
干系人 Stakeholders
?
可能影响项目或受项目影响的个人或团体。
Individuals/groups who may affect or be affected by the project.
🧍
经验总结 Lessons Learned
?
在项目过程中获得的知识，指导未来如何应对类似情况。
Knowledge gained during the project showing how events should be addressed in the future.
💡
